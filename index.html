/ 01 · About

Mastering the era of
Generative Models.

Generative AI is reshaping industries, from creative arts to complex software engineering. This comprehensive 15-day summer school is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Organized by the Department of CST & CSE(AIML) at JIS College of Engineering, participants will engage in hands-on workshops, build live projects, and interact with leading industry experts.

/ 03 · Curriculum

Detailed Syllabus.

Module 01

Module 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and Gen AI

  • Evolution of Artificial Intelligence
  • Introduction to Generative AI
  • Traditional AI vs Generative AI
  • Applications of Gen AI
  • Future Scope and Industry Trends
Module 02

Module 2: Fundamentals of Machine Learning and Deep Learning

  • Basics of Machine Learning
  • Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  • NLP Fundamentals
  • Transformers and Attention Mechanism
  • Introduction to Foundation Models
Module 03

Module 3: Large Language Models (LLMs)

  • Understanding LLM Architecture
  • GPT, Gemini, Claude, LLaMA, and Open-source Models
  • Tokenization and Embeddings
  • Fine-tuning Concepts
  • Limitations and Challenges of LLMs
Module 04

Module 4: Prompt Engineering

  • Basics of Prompt Engineering
  • Zero-shot and Few-shot Prompting
  • Chain-of-Thought Prompting
  • Role-based Prompting
  • Prompt Optimization Techniques
  • Hands-on Prompt Design
Module 05

Module 5: AI Chatbots and Conversational AI

  • Evolution of Chatbots
  • Designing Conversational Flows
  • Building Chatbots using APIs
  • LangChain and Vector Databases
  • Deploying AI Assistants
Module 06

Module 6: Text Generation and NLP Applications

  • Content Creation (Blogs, Articles)
  • Summarization and Translation
  • Code Generation and Debugging
  • Sentiment Analysis and Text Classification
Module 07

Module 7: Image Generation Models

  • Introduction to DALL-E, MidJourney, Stable Diffusion
  • Text-to-Image Generation
  • Image-to-Image Translation
  • Prompting for Visuals
  • Ethical Concerns in Image Gen AI
Module 08

Module 8: Real-World Applications of Gen AI

  • Gen AI in Healthcare
  • Gen AI in Finance
  • Gen AI in Marketing and Design
  • Gen AI in Software Development
  • Case Studies
Module 09

Module 9: Hands-on Projects

  • Project 1: Custom AI Chatbot
  • Project 2: Content Generation Tool
  • Project 3: AI-powered Image Editor
Module 10

Module 10: AI Ethics, Security, and Future Directions

  • Bias and Fairness in AI
  • Deepfakes and Misinformation
  • Data Privacy and Security
  • Regulations and Guidelines
  • The Future of AGI (Artificial General Intelligence)
Module 11

Module 11: Research and Publication Guidance

  • Formulating Research Problems in AI
  • Literature Review Techniques
  • Drafting Research Papers
  • Identifying Reputed Journals (SCI/Scopus)
  • Responding to Reviewer Comments
Module 12

Module 12: Capstone Project and Evaluation

  • Project Presentation
  • Evaluation and Feedback
  • Q&A Session
  • Certificate Distribution
  • Valedictory Ceremony
